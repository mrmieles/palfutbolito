About Us

High Quality NFT Collection

Pal’ Futbolito NFT is an NFT collection of 2,022 pieces that have been inspired in the Soccer World Cup that will take place in Qatar, 2022.

These NFTs are not simple ones, they have been created to help the soccer community gain knowledge when it comes to cryptocurrency and NFT investments. Having these pieces will grant access to NFT education and soccer clinics with Puerto Rico’s most talented female soccer players. Other than this, as a HOLDER, you will have access to a monthly soccer game and many giveaways.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What’s the supply?

The supply of Pal’ Futbolito NFT collection is of 2,022. This number was chosen to honor the Qatar 2022 FIFA World Cup that will take place during November of this year.

Which network will the collection be sold on?

The blockchain that will be used to mint and sell the collection will be the Polygon network.

How would I be able to get on the Allowlist of the collection?

Getting a spot on the Allowlist of the collection is extremely easy. The only thing that you would have to do is follow us in our social media accounts (handle: @palfutbolitonft) We hold giveaways in which an Allowlist spot is usually a prize!

What marketplace will Pal’ Futbolito NFT collection be listed on?

The marketplace that we would be using as secondary market will be OpenSea. We would have the MINT done in a specific webpage that will be used for that given purpose.

What’s the purpose of the collection?
