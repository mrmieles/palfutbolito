About Us

High Quality NFT Collection

This digital art collection has been founded by a former soccer player who’s main focus is to help out soccer clubs in Puerto Rico with monetary and material donations as well. The previously mentioned action will also come with a mural painted by Puerto Rican artists in one of the walls of Academia Quintana F.C. soccer field. This mural will be commenced during the second phase of the project, and it will be dedicated to female soccer players who have represented Puerto Rico in it’s national selection.

Pa’l Futbolito NFT is also working with female players who are known for their field performance and team playing skills. Karina Socarrás, Laura Suarez, Mirianeé “Nené” Zaragoza and Adriana “Tuti” Tirado are serving as our ambassadors because they are fully invested in the great labor that this collection has been planning and working with.
